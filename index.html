Welcome to Julie’s Pet Scanning & Microchipping

At Julie’s Pet Scanning and Microchipping, we offer top-notch pet ultrasound scanning and microchipping services throughout Neath, Port Talbot, Swansea, and Aberdare. Our advanced ultrasound technology ensures a safe, non-invasive way to monitor your pet’s health and confirm pregnancies. Our experienced team provides a compassionate approach, making the process stress-free for both you and your pet.

We understand that your pet’s safety is paramount. Our microchipping service ensures your furry friend is easily identifiable and can be quickly returned if they ever go missing. Complying with UK law, our microchipping is carried out with precision and care. Enjoy peace of mind knowing that your pet is protected with a permanent and unique ID.

Our mobile services bring the latest technology right to your doorstep. We strive to make each visit as comfortable and convenient as possible, ensuring your pet receives the best care in a familiar environment. Trust Julie’s Pet Scanning and Microchipping for exceptional service and support for your pet’s health and safety.

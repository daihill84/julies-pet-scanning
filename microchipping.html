Microchipping Services

At Julie’s Pet Scanning and Microchipping, we offer comprehensive microchipping services across Neath, Port Talbot, Swansea, and Aberdare. Microchipping is a crucial step in ensuring the safety of your pets, and it is a legal requirement for dogs in the UK. Our mobile microchipping service is designed to be convenient and stress-free, bringing the service directly to your home.

Our expert team ensures that each microchip is properly implanted and registered with your contact details. This guarantees that your pet can be easily identified and reunited with you if they ever become lost. We prioritize your pet’s comfort and safety throughout the microchipping process, making it as smooth and efficient as possible.

With Julie’s Pet Scanning and Microchipping, you can have peace of mind knowing that your pet is protected by a reliable identification system. Our mobile service means you don’t have to worry about transporting your pet; we come to you, making the process easy and convenient.
